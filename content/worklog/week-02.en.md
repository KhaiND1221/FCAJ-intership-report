### Challenges & Lessons

* **Challenges:**
  * VPC routing concepts (Subnet routing, CIDR blocks, NAT Gateways vs IGW) are abstract and challenging to configure correctly without breaking connectivity.
  * Writing custom JSON policies for IAM is error-prone.

* **Solutions:**
  * Used architectural diagramming tools to visualize packet flows across subnets before provisioning on AWS.
  * Utilized the AWS IAM Policy Simulator to test policy actions effectively.

* **Lessons Learned:**
  * Networking and IAM are the ultimate core of cloud security. Without properly isolating VPCs and strictly controlling IAM roles, higher-level services are inherently vulnerable.
